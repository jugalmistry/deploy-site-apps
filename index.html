The Challenge

In Mumbai's underserved communities, 70% of students lack access to computers at home, creating a digital divide that limits their exposure to technology careers and perpetuates cycles of limited opportunities.

Problem Statement

How might we design physical-digital learning experiences that make coding and game design accessible, engaging, and relevant for students who lack traditional tech resources?

My Role

  • VP of Design & Co-founder

    Led design vision and strategy

  • Lead UX Researcher

    Conducted ethnographic research

  • Product Designer

    Designed physical and digital components

  • Workshop Facilitator

    Led co-creation sessions

Infrastructure

Stakeholders

Resources

Cost sensitivity, limited funding, sustainability requirements

Scale

Motivational Factors

Hands-on activities crucial, game creation resonated, peer collaboration essential

Parent Perspectives

Career outcomes valued, cost is primary barrier, trust requires visible progress

Teacher Capacity

Limited tech training, 30+ students per class, minimal prep time available

→

Design Decisions

Offline-First Physical Kits

No internet dependency, tactile learning components, QR codes for optional content

Group activities by default, peer teaching encouraged, shared device strategies

Visible Progress System

AI-Enhanced Acadnion: Technical Details

🔒 Privacy-First Architecture

All AI processing happens locally on affordable Raspberry Pi clusters. No student data ever leaves the school premises.

🧠 Intelligent Features

  • Adaptive Hints: AI observes struggle patterns and provides contextual help
  • Voice Commands: Students can ask questions in their native language
  • Progress Prediction: Early warning system for intervention
  • Peer Matching: AI pairs students for optimal collaboration

📊 Implementation Roadmap

  1. Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Voice assistant for basic commands
  2. Phase 2 (Months 4-6): Adaptive hint system
  3. Phase 3 (Months 7-9): Predictive analytics dashboard
  4. Phase 4 (Months 10-12): Full AI companion
